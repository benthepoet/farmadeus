Farm Log 2020

Published: Sat 11 January 2020
Updated: Sun 26 January 2020
By Ben Hanna

In Logs.

February 11, 2020

Siberian Crab Apple seedlings have started emerging.

Removed five Anatovka Apple seeds from refrigerator and planted in a can. Placed 50 Hardy Kiwi, 25 Juneberry, and 25 Saskatoon seeds in the refrigerator for stratification.

February 8, 2020

Checked on the apple seeds in the refrigerator. Planted five Siberian Crab Apple seeds that had sprouted.

January 26, 2020

The Dunstan Chestnuts have begun to sprout in the refrigerator. One was compromised by mold. Pulled two out and planted in containers.

January 11, 2020

The hens have started laying. Collected three medium light-brown eggs today. I'm guessing they're from the Golden Laced Wyandotte hens as I've read Welsummer eggs tend to be speckled.
